Lumbini

Lumbini, nestled in the Rupandehi District of Nepal, stands as a revered pilgrimage site for millions worldwide. It is renowned as the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama, who later became Gautama Buddha, the founder of Buddhism.

Lumbini

Lumbini, nestled in Nepal's tranquil landscape, is a revered destination embodying the essence of Buddhism. Home to the sacred Maya Devi Temple, it offers a profound pilgrimage experience beyond being the birthplace of Lord Buddha. Lumbini's serene gardens, ancient monasteries, and meditation sites inspire seekers of spiritual wisdom and cultural heritage. Visitors from diverse backgrounds come to immerse themselves in the profound teachings and peaceful ambiance that define this sacred sanctuary.

Things To Do In Lumbini

Visit the Sacred Garden and Maya Devi Temple

The Maya Devi Temple stands as the spiritual heart of Lumbini, encapsulating the profound significance of Siddhartha Gautama's birthplace.

Explore the Monastic Zone

The Monastic Zone within Lumbini is a captivating landscape that showcases the global diversity and unity of Buddhist traditions.
